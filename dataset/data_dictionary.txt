KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIs)
================================================================================

Total Revenue
-------------
Description: Total revenue across all markets and time periods
Value: ₹31.9 Billion
Unit: Indian Rupees (Billions)
Calculation: Sum of revenue before 5G + after 5G

ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
-------------------------------
Description: Average revenue generated per active user
Value: ₹200.7
Unit: Indian Rupees
Calculation: Total Revenue / Total Active Users

TAU (Total Active Users)
------------------------
Description: Total number of active subscribers
Value: 161.7 Million
Unit: Million users
Note: Represents active user base across all markets

TUsU (Total Unsubscribed Users)
-------------------------------
Description: Number of users who unsubscribed post-5G launch
Value: 12.6 Million
Unit: Million users
Insight: Indicates customer churn concern

Revenue Change %
----------------
Description: Percentage change in revenue after 5G launch
Value: -0.50%
Unit: Percentage
Calculation: ((After 5G - Before 5G) / Before 5G) × 100
Status: UNDERPERFORMING (negative growth)

Before 5G Revenue
-----------------
Description: Total revenue before 5G launch
Value: ₹16.0 Billion
Unit: Indian Rupees (Billions)
Period: Pre-5G launch period

After 5G Revenue
----------------
Description: Total revenue after 5G launch
Value: ₹15.9 Billion
Unit: Indian Rupees (Billions)
Period: Post-5G launch period

Monthly Average Revenue (MA)
----------------------------
Description: Average monthly revenue
Value: ₹4.0 Billion
Unit: Indian Rupees (Billions)
Calculation: Total Revenue / Number of months

================================================================================
CALCULATED METRICS
================================================================================

Revenue Change (Absolute)
-------------------------
Formula: After 5G Revenue - Before 5G Revenue
Overall: ₹15.9B - ₹16.0B = -₹0.1B

Revenue Change (Percentage)
---------------------------
Formula: ((After - Before) / Before) × 100
Overall: ((15.9 - 16.0) / 16.0) × 100 = -0.50%

Churn Rate
----------
Formula: (Unsubscribed Users / Total Active Users) × 100
Calculation: (12.6M / 161.7M) × 100 = 7.79%

Market Concentration
--------------------
Top 5 Cities Revenue Share: ~60% of total revenue
Mumbai alone: ~15% of total revenue
